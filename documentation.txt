WHAT IS A CHATBOT?
-A chatbot or chatterbot is a software application used to conduct an on-line chat conversation via text or text-to-speech, 
in lieu of providing direct contact with a live human agent.
-it is a type of software that can help customers by automating conversations and interact with them through messaging platforms.

APPLICATION OF CHATBOTS:
     -Messaging Apps(even on SMS)
     -Company Internal Platforms(e.g Customer Support): companies use online assistants  
                                                       instead of human call centers
     -Healthcare: chatbots would be useful for setting up appointments,
                  locating health clinics, or providing medication information.
